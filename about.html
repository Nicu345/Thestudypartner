What we do

We present programs from multiple institutions in a consistent format so visitors can compare options quickly. When someone requests a brochure, your team can follow up and guide them through the application process.

Partner institutions (examples)

  • DTI University (via POBS) — PhDr / PaedDr distance programs
  • Brand University — MBA Brand Management
  • Simpson University — MBA and MA Community Counseling
  • Anderson University — Ph.D. programs in Leadership and Preaching

How it works

  1. Browse and filter programs by degree, subject area, institution, and format.
  2. Open a program page for details and highlights.
  3. Request a brochure (name, email, phone). Your CRM automates follow-up.

Trust & transparency

  • Show tuition clearly (total or per credit), with year-to-year notes.
  • Link to official university pages from “Learn more” when ready.
  • Use logos/badges only with partner approval.
