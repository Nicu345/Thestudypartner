FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Request brochure

Do you award the degrees?

No. Degrees are awarded by the partner universities. We provide the marketplace experience and brochure request flow.

Is everything 100% online?

Many programs are fully online; some can be hybrid. Use the format filter in the catalog.

Can tuition change?

Yes. Tuition can change by academic year. Always confirm final details with the university.

How does the brochure request work?

You enter your contact details. Your team (or automation) sends program materials and offers a short consultation.

Do you offer scholarships?

Scholarships depend on each university. Add official links and eligibility rules program-by-program.

What do I need to apply?

Requirements vary (degrees, transcripts, references, writing sample, experience). See each program page for a checklist.
